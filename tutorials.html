FaceTime

1. Go to Finder and double cick.
2. Look through the applications and double click on FaceTime .
3. Click on the search bar on the top and enter a contact name.
4. Click on the video call button .
5. To end the call, click the red X .
6. To mute yourself in the call, click the microphone button .
7. To stop your video in the call, click the no camera button .
8. To expand your screen, click on the expand button .
9. To see your list of contacts and recent calls, click the menu button .
10. To close the application, click on the red button in the top left corner .
